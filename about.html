About SyTech

25 Years of Bringing Structure to Public Sector Records.

SyTech is a services-first firm that helps public agencies modernize records management without adding new software. We have digitized over 800 million records and built our practice around partnership, not licensing.

Our Story

From Paper to Cloud, One Agency at a Time.

For more than two decades, SyTech has worked alongside California school districts, county offices, and public agencies to move information out of file rooms and into structured, searchable, cloud-native systems. We have seen every variation of records chaos, and we know how to bring it to clarity.

What sets us apart is the scale and substance of our experience. With 800+ million records digitized and 25+ years in the public sector, we know how to bring structure to unstructured data in ways most firms only theorize about.

How We Are Built

Two Teams, One Mission.

SyTech is structured around two internal teams that work together to deliver every engagement. One handles the hands-on work of moving information. The other builds the systems that make that information useful for years to come.

01 — Services Team

Hands-On Records Work

Our Services Team handles the heavy lifting: scanning, structuring, and migrating information from paper and legacy systems into modern cloud platforms. They are the practitioners who turn clutter into compliant, indexed records.

02 — Development Team

Cloud-Native Tools

Our Development Team builds scalable tools that replace outdated software, using the cloud platforms our clients already own. Forms, workflows, and automation that live inside Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace, not on a vendor server.
