About SyTech

25 Years of Bringing Structure to Public Sector Records.

SyTech is a services-first firm that helps public agencies modernize records management without adding new software. Since 2000, we have built our practice around partnership, not licensing.

Our Story

From Paper to Cloud, One Agency at a Time.

Since 2000, SyTech has provided digital transformation services for hundreds of public agencies across California — school districts, county offices, statewide departments. We have never been in the business of reselling proprietary software. From the start, our work has been to help agencies move their records and data into open, extensible cloud platforms — and to spend their budgets on outcomes instead of licenses.

Twenty-five years later, that approach has produced 4,579 active cloud clients, 10.7 million records under management, and a client roster that includes some of the largest public agencies in the state.

Cloud Solutions
A Cloud-First Practice — Since 2002

We Built Our First Cloud Deployment Before "Cloud" Was the Word for It.

In 2002, we built our first native cloud records management solution for Stockton Unified School District — years before the rest of the industry moved off on-premise servers. That early bet shaped everything that followed.

Since then, we have built thousands of cloud-based records and data services for public agencies across California, including Los Angeles County, the California Department of Child Support Services, the California Department of Natural Resources, and Yolo County District Attorney's Office. Open cloud was the right path then. It is the only path now.

How We Are Built

Two Teams, One Mission.

SyTech is structured around two internal teams that work together to deliver every engagement. One handles the hands-on work of moving information. The other builds the systems that make that information useful for years to come.

01 — Services Team

Hands-On Records Work

Our Services Team handles the heavy lifting: scanning, structuring, and migrating information from paper and legacy systems into modern cloud platforms. They are the practitioners who turn clutter into compliant, indexed records.

02 — Development Team

Cloud-Native Tools

Our Development Team builds scalable tools that replace outdated software, using the cloud platforms our clients already own. Forms, workflows, and automation that live inside Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace, not on a vendor server.

Our Mission

From Complexity to Clarity.

SyTech delivers practical, future-ready services that reduce complexity, lower costs, and help public agencies work smarter. Through our proven approach — Organize. Unify. Scale. — we help organizations move confidently toward the future of open cloud, automation, and AI.
