The Pipeline

Four Steps. One Indexed Archive.

What happens from the moment a legacy archive enters the pipeline until every record sits indexed and searchable inside your tenant.

  1. 01
    Read

    Each record is processed — OCR for paper, text extraction for digital files.

  2. 02
    Classify

    Records sorted by type, department, and retention — against your taxonomy.

  3. 03
    Index

    Metadata is extracted and written to a searchable index your staff can query.

  4. 04
    Deliver

    Records land in your SharePoint environment, governed by your existing permissions and policies.

The Lift

The Work, Without the Years.

Indexing a legacy archive by hand is a months-or-years project staffed by humans. The AI ingestion tool does the same work at machine pace.

Manual Indexing

Staff open each record, read it, classify it, and enter metadata by hand. The project takes months or years. Accuracy depends on the day.

AI Ingestion

The tool reads, classifies, and indexes each record against your taxonomy at machine pace. Accuracy is consistent. Your staff supervise the process.
