Security Overview

Built to Protect What Agencies Can't Afford to Lose.

Public records carry real consequences when they're exposed, lost, or mishandled. We've spent 25 years building our work around that reality. Security at SyTech isn't a single feature — it's how the whole operation is designed, from where your data lives to who is allowed near it.

Data Sovereignty Isn't Just Digital.

Your records live in your cloud tenant — that is the digital half. The physical half is where the scanning, indexing, and chain-of-custody work happens on the way in. Both are governed by the same discipline.

The Dossier

Every Domain, Documented.

The strip above is the at-a-glance view. Here is the detail behind each domain — the people, the place, the way records move and are destroyed, the insurance, and the cloud underneath it all.

The People

Trained, Vetted, and Under Agreement.

Our staff complete a regular training program covering HIPAA, the Sarbanes-Oxley Act, and California and Federal records law, and every one of them works under a signed confidentiality agreement. The standard that protects your data digitally protects it in person, too — the people touching your records are held to the same discipline as the systems that store them.

The Facility

A Facility Built for Record Security.

The physical work of scanning, indexing, and chain-of-custody happens in a stand-alone building designed for record and data security from the ground up, by a consultant who designs security facilities for banks. Electronic access control. Biometric fingerprint readers on sensitive areas. 32 security cameras covering every critical area, inside and out. Your records are never left unattended, and only authorized, agreement-bound staff are allowed near them.

In Transit & At Rest

Protected, Coming and Going.

We encrypt communications and protect every login, and we monitor our hosting network daily for uptime and security. When physical records are transported, our vehicles are GPS-tracked, and customer records are never left unattended in an unsecured vehicle. Movement and storage are governed by the same chain-of-custody discipline.

Destruction

When Records Are Destroyed, You Get Proof.

When the time comes to destroy records, we shred on-site in a manner that exceeds DoD standards, with the process recorded on camera, and we issue a certificate of destruction for your files. You're never left wondering whether something was actually disposed of — you have documentation that it was, done to a defined standard.

Insurance

Insured to Carry the Risk With You.

SyTech carries a specific rider on its Errors & Omissions policy covering network security and data privacy. On request, we'll name your agency as an Additional Insured on our policies. The protection isn't just a promise on a webpage — it's written into the policy itself.

Cloud Infrastructure

Backed by Enterprise-Grade Cloud Infrastructure.

Because we build inside Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace, your records sit on the same infrastructure those providers secure for enterprises and governments worldwide — with their redundancy and physical data-center security underneath everything we build. Our 1DocStop platform is deployed in Microsoft Azure Government.

If anything here raises a question for your agency, ask us directly. We're glad to walk through our security practices before you ever share a record.
