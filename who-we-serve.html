The Two Personas

We Meet You Where You Are.

Most public agencies fall into one of two camps when they come to SyTech. We work with both — and the path forward looks different for each.

Persona 1

Still Using Paper

Paper forms still drive many of your business processes. Forms move slowly. Approvals take time. Staff re-enter data into different systems. Records are hard to track and harder to audit.

You know modernization is overdue, but you are wary of adding another piece of software to manage. SyTech meets you here — we digitize, structure, and migrate your records into the cloud platform you already own. No new license. No second login.

Common starting point: file rooms full of binders, decades of historical records, compliance pressure rising.
Persona 2

Already Using E-Forms Software

You have adopted electronic forms tools, but they are costly and disconnected. Your current platform sits outside your records system, creates silos, and does not connect to the workflows you actually need.

You are looking for a better path that leverages the cloud investments you have already made. SyTech replaces standalone tools with a unified ecosystem inside your existing Microsoft 365 or Google environment — fewer vendors, lower costs, more control.

Common starting point: paying for a SaaS forms tool that is not solving the underlying records problem.
