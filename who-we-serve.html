The Two Personas

We Meet You Where You Are.

Most public agencies fall into one of two camps when they come to SyTech. We work with both — and the path forward looks different for each.

Persona 1

Still Using Paper

Paper forms still drive many of your business processes. Forms move slowly. Approvals take time. Staff re-enter data into different systems. Records are hard to track and harder to audit.

You know modernization is overdue, but you are wary of adding another piece of software to manage. SyTech meets you here — we digitize, structure, and migrate your records into the cloud platform you already own. No new license. No second login.

Common starting point: file rooms full of binders, decades of historical records, compliance pressure rising.
Persona 2

Already Using E-Forms Software

You have adopted electronic forms tools, but they are costly and disconnected. Your current platform sits outside your records system, creates silos, and does not connect to the workflows you actually need.

You are looking for a better path that leverages the cloud investments you have already made. SyTech replaces standalone tools with a unified ecosystem inside your existing Microsoft 365 or Google environment — fewer vendors, lower costs, more control.

Common starting point: paying for a SaaS forms tool that is not solving the underlying records problem.
Work We're Proud Of

Real Agencies. Real Outcomes.

A short sample of the public agencies we have built records and data services for. Each engagement looked different. The pattern underneath was the same — meet the agency where they were, build inside what they already owned, and leave them with a system they could run for years.

California Natural Resources Agency

Statewide Paper Records Digitization

SharePoint Online + Microsoft Stack
50MRecords 15,000+Boxes 2,000Doc Types 13Departments

A two-year deadline, a building move, and decades of paper across 13 departments. SyTech digitized 50 million records from 15,000+ boxes spanning 2,000 document types — and built records management on the agency's existing Microsoft 365. Likely the largest SharePoint Online and Microsoft Stack deployment for a California public agency.

California State Lands Commission

OSCAR

Online System for Customer Applications and Records
4.5MAcres 200+Years 4M+Documents 160Record Types

The Commission manages 4.5 million acres and 200+ years of records — 4 million-plus documents across 160 record types, all on paper. SyTech built OSCAR, a low-code system on SharePoint Online and the Microsoft stack that modernized leasing and permitting and put two centuries of records within reach.

Yolo & Santa Barbara Counties

eSCARS

Electronic Suspected Child Abuse Report System

A cloud reporting system that lets jurisdictions share suspected child-abuse reports and spot patterns that cross county lines — patterns that used to slip through the gaps. Built secure, built to be shared. More California agencies have since joined the system.

Yolo County District Attorney's Office

The First Paperless DA Office

Case File Digitization
40%Staff Time Freed 1stPaperless DA in CA

Staff were spending nearly 40% of their time moving physical case files — files that got lost, and that only one person could use at a time. SyTech digitized the operation and made Yolo the first DA office in California to go fully paperless: multi-user access, OCR keyword search, and nightly off-site backup.
