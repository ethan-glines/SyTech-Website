Five Differentiators

What Makes SyTech Different.

Public agencies have plenty of options for records and forms software. SyTech's approach is structured around five core differentiators that change the economics, security posture, and long-term flexibility of every engagement.

01 — PLATFORM APPROACH

No New Software Required

SyTech builds inside Microsoft 365 and Google Workspace — platforms your agency already owns and funds. There is no new app to procure, license, or maintain.

02 — DATA SOVEREIGNTY

Your Data Stays in Your Cloud

No vendor-controlled servers. Your records, forms, and workflows live in your agency's own cloud tenant. Your security policies govern your data — always.

03 — SERVICES MODEL

Services, Not Licenses

SyTech is a partnership, not a subscription. We combine connective tools with hands-on implementation, workflow configuration, and ongoing support — structured around outcomes, not seat counts.

04 — FUTURE-PROOF

Native AI and Automation

Because we build inside your existing cloud, you get native access to Microsoft Copilot, Power Automate, and Azure capabilities — no vendor add-ons, no custom integrations to maintain.

05 — PROVEN AT SCALE

25+ Years of Experience

Over 800 million records digitized. Deep public sector expertise across school districts, county offices, and statewide agencies. We have done this work at every scale.
